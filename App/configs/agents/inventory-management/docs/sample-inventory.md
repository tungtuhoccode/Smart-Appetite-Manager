# Sample Home Inventory

A realistic home kitchen inventory for demo and testing. Copy-paste the items below into the Inventory Assistant chat to populate the database.

---

## Quick Add (single prompt)

Paste this into the Inventory Assistant chat:

```
Add the following items to my inventory:
- 2 kg chicken breast
- 1 kg ground beef
- 500 g salmon fillet
- 12 eggs
- 2 L whole milk
- 500 g cheddar cheese
- 250 g butter
- 500 mL plain yogurt
- 2 kg white rice
- 500 g spaghetti
- 1 loaf whole wheat bread
- 500 g all-purpose flour
- 3 yellow onions
- 1 head garlic
- 4 potatoes
- 3 carrots
- 2 bell peppers
- 1 head broccoli
- 200 g baby spinach
- 3 tomatoes
- 2 lemons
- 1 bunch bananas (5 count)
- 2 apples
- 1 can diced tomatoes (400 g)
- 1 can coconut milk (400 mL)
- 1 can black beans (540 mL)
- 1 can chickpeas (540 mL)
- 500 mL olive oil
- 250 mL soy sauce
- 100 g tomato paste
- salt (1 kg)
- black pepper (50 g)
- paprika (40 g)
- cumin (35 g)
- dried oregano (25 g)
- chili flakes (20 g)
- 1 L chicken broth
- 250 mL maple syrup
- 500 g granulated sugar
- 100 g dark chocolate chips
```

## Missing ingredient scenarios for testing

After populating inventory, try these recipe prompts to generate shopping lists with missing ingredients:

| Recipe idea                  | Likely missing items                        |
|------------------------------|---------------------------------------------|
| Beef tacos                   | Taco shells, sour cream, lettuce, salsa     |
| Chicken stir-fry             | Sesame oil, ginger, cornstarch, green onions |
| Salmon teriyaki bowl         | Mirin, rice vinegar, avocado, edamame       |
| Spaghetti carbonara          | Pancetta/bacon, parmesan cheese, heavy cream |
| Chocolate chip cookies       | Vanilla extract, baking soda, brown sugar   |
| Chickpea curry               | Garam masala, ginger, naan bread, cilantro  |

These missing items are good candidates for testing the Shopping page / Flipp deal search.
